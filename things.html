Things I Own

Created: Thu 20 December 2018 in Personal
#random #nickwinter

This post was inspired by Nick Winter's "99 Things" post. (I recommend you give it a read). After reading Nick's post and contemplating my situation, I feel like I have a good amount of stuff that I don't need. It's not efficient to own things that I'm not going to use. Plus, since I'm moving to the bay area soon, I want to avoid clutter in my future space :p.

How to tackle this? Let's start by deciding on a number of things to own. I choose 100. I will omit consumables and count most things that are bundled together as one.
